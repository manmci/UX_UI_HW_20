I Haven't Played Earthbound


One Page Risograph Zine

Procreate, 2019

The title is pretty self-explanatory. This was inspired by watching my wife play the classic video game, Earthbound. The inside features 6 of my favorite monsters from the game, while the back cover shows the delightfully bizarre Mr. Saturn character.

This was designed to be riograph printed (4 colors) on light yellow paper, but I have not been able to yet due to the ongoing pandemic.
