Nightcrawler


Illustration

Procreate, 2020

Inspired by the Judas Priest song of the same name and Boris Vallejo's movie poster for White Buffalo, I wanted to try and capture the ferocity of one of the beasts that prowls my imagination.

I also wanted to force myself to practice adding a background that framed the subject without distracting from it.
